Generate Gifts

Create gift codes when you don’t know what email address to use. e.g., anonymous juries or bundle sales. If you want to send an invitation directly to a known email address, use the new invitation page instead.

Generate only as many gift codes as you need. You will be redirected to a list of all of the gifts you have created when the generation is finished.
